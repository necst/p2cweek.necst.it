Multiple Paper Registration


If you have to register more than one paper, you are entitled to have the following discount:

Full paper registrations to EUC or ISPA
  • Two paper to register: one full registration to a single EUC or ISPA conference and a 15% discount on the second full registration for EUC or ISPA
  • Three paper to register: one full registration to a single EUC or ISPA conference and a 25% discount on the second and the third full registrations for EUC or ISPA.
Full paper registrations to P2CWEEK
  • More then one paper to register: one full registration to P2CWEEK and a 50% discount on all the other full registrations to the EUC or ISPA.

What to do
