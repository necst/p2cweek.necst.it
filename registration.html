Registration


Early registration will be possible in few days! Stay tuned!

Different registration packs are available within the P2C Week. In fact, it is possible to register to the full P2C Week event or to only the EUC Conference or the ISPA Conference. Within this page is possible to find all the information you need about the regiration. If you have any doubt, please contact us.


At P2C we will have the following "facilities":

  1. Wine and Cheese event @ Politecnico di Milano
  2. Visit, in group of 20 people with a guide, to the Cenacolo (The Last Supper)
  3. Social event (to be announced)
  4. Lunch and 2 coffee break per day

Full P2C Week Registration


For the entire (full access) P2C event the registration costs are:

Registration Type Early/Author Fees Late/On-Site Fees
Members 650 € 750 €
Non-members 750 € 850 €
Student members 450 € 550 €
Student non-members 750 € 650 €

A full registration to P2C will cover (a), (b), (c), and (d) from monday to friday. A student registration to P2C will cover (a), (c), and (d) from monday to friday.

Other costs


Optional events pricing

If you want to add events to your subscription, please consider that:

  • Ticket to visit the Cenacolo (The Last Supper): 50 €
  • Ticket for the social event: 100 €

Note that the aforementioned addional prices are not useful in the case of the full P2C Week registration, since these events are alredy included.


Multiple papers registration

If you published more then one paper, here you can find the pricing information:

  • Full registration for the 1st paper
  • Full reg. with a discount of the 10% on the 2nd paper (ONLY on the 2nd)
  • Full reg. with a discount of the 25% on the 3rd paper (both on the 2nd and the 3rd)

Additional paper pages

If you are registering an accepted papers, you have to consider that for both EUC and ISPA the number of pages is 8. It is possible to publish up to 10-pages papers with an additional fee of 100€ per additional page.



Important notes

While choosing your favorite subscription, please consider that:

  • Each paper has to have a full registration (to the corresponding conference).
  • A registration to P2C counts also as a full registration for EUC or ISPA for the paper.
  • Student registration does not include the ticket for the Cenacolo (The Last Supper), while the social event is included
