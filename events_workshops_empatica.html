Empatica Workshop


Human data analysis with Empatica and live testing APIs


Intro Empatica

Empatica is an affective computing company, focused on human data analytics. We develop groundbreaking wearable devices with medical quality sensing. Our goal is to help people make better decisions in their everyday lives based on smart human data analytics.
We design and develop the worldâ€™s smallest and most accurate wearable device for medical research of human behavior in daily life. Our first product, E3, is used in 30 countries around the world, its clients are the most respected hospitals, universities and companies that use it for advanced research on human behavior. It provides the most sophisticated way to monitor autonomic nervous system disruption and heart rate variability, among a set of 5 sensors.

About the Workshop

The workshop would like to familiarize the audience with our tools, both the E3 and the APIs. Data about human physiology and behavior have never been available before in a massive and continuous scale. We will show the audience the potential and ease for the collection with our device. It will also be an hands-on experience to tinker with E3 bracelets with live testing. The workshop will be held by Alberto Guarino, Senior Software Developer at Empatica.

Agenda

The Empatica Workshop has been rescheduled to Friday 29 Aug. The workshop starts at 15.30.
